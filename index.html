This unofficial podcast is based primarily on publicly available sources, supplemented by a small number of informal conversations with people who were there. It is intended as a concise, audience-friendly telling of Madrigal’s story. This effort is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or produced by Madrigal Pharmaceuticals Inc.; it is one employee’s attempt to piece together and tell the story in a clear, human way.

Artistic license has been taken for narrative flow, but the story is generally grounded in the public record. Most of the dialogue is reconstructed from memory and secondhand accounts. This should be understood as a storytelling device rather than verbatim quotation. Voices are performed by actors; no actual colleagues are speaking. AI voices are nearly there, but for lines in a story like this, humans still do it better (at least in 2026), which is why they got the parts.
